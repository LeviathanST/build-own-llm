# Build Your Own LLM Workshop — Written Chapters

This repository is a **faithful textual transformation** of Justin Angel's excellent [Build Your Own LLM Workshop](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLweJS2YZCfkeXXdfCKGaxAhm2w8p0u1z6) video series.

23 hours of video → 23 readable chapters. Same content, same structure, same sequence. No fluff, no filler — just the workshop in text form, with every key claim backed by a `[MM:SS]` timestamp back to the original.

## Why this exists

The videos are dense. Really dense. Pausing, rewinding, and searching through 58-minute talks to find that one formula is painful. These chapters give you the same material at your own pace — read, skim, search, and jump straight to what you need.

## How these chapters were made

The raw transcripts were extracted via YouTube's API. Each was then carefully rewritten — preserving every technical detail, formula, exercise, and demonstration — while removing speech artifacts, fragmented sentences, and filler that makes transcripts hard to read. The result reads like a textbook chapter, not a recording.
